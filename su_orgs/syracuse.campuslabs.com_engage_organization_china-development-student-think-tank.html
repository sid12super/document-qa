China Development Student Think Tank (Syracuse University Chapter)

About

The mission of the China Development Student Think Tank (Syracuse Chapter) is to provide constructive academic reports and intellectual ideas regarding Chinese economic development, political reform, cultural diversity, and diplomatic strategy to students interested in China and Sino-U.S. relations. All missions will be provided by the executive board of China Development Student Think Tank (Syracuse Chapter) and its higher authority, China Development Student Think Tank (North America Committee) of China Development Forum, one of the most influential non-government think tanks in Beijing, China.

Additional Information

Description:
China Development Student Think Tank (CDSTT) is a non-governmental student-run organization for communication and research. Its core concentration lies in China's politics & economy, U.S.-Sino relationship, and Asian-Pacific sustainable development. To facilitate the understanding in the topics, the organization provides unique opportunities of research, mentorship, and internship to its members in American and Chinese colleges. Established since 2014, CDSTT now has officially recognized chapters in Syracuse University and George Washington University, and student ambassador teams in Georgetown University, Harvard University and Johns Hopkins University.
