Additional Information

Description:
The vision of Loud & Clear is to create music video content that will both help student artists and students interested in studio production create a portfolio that showcases professional works. Through hands-on practice and a collaborative working environment, students will understand how to properly pair their visual media and storytelling skills with practical skills like camera operation, technical direction, and executive production. Any student who is a musician and interested in studio performance is welcome to join our community. Similarly, any student who is interested in production - even if it is not solely studio production - is welcome to join. Our mission is to provide a positive, holistic experience for students working on a professional studio production set through hands-on learning, in-person collaboration, and a collective effort for higher level content.
