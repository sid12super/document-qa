About

NO EXPERIENCE? NO EQUIPMENT? NO WORRIES!
We welcome new and experienced fencers to join. If you've never fenced before, we'll teach you all the basics!

Our club has all the equipment you'll need to practice, but you're encouraged to bring your own equipment if you have it, and we can store it securely.

We do not currently charge dues or membership fees, but that is subject to change.

 

TOURNAMENTS & COMPETITIONS
Upon sufficient interest, we'll attend nearby fencing competitions. Members pay their own individual entrance fees, which typically range from $10-20 per competition (but you may be reimbursed if our club budget allows). You aren't required to compete, but it's a great way to test your skill!

 

FALL '22 PRACTICE TIMES
Tuesdays 7:30-10pm, Fridays & Sundays 3-6 PM

Attendance is not mandatory. You're welcome to show up to as many practices as you like--but remember, you'll get out of it what you put in!
